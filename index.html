Financial Modelling Bootcamp

This bootcamp is attended by the Finance Club of IIT Bombay during winter break. This is a one month long bootcamp wherein we were taught about various topics in Finance like future-and-options trading, algorithmic trading. Also, we learnt about how to read the financial statements and also the reports of a company. A detailed study on stocks of a company for about 10 years is studied and a final detailed report is made. We also made 3-statement model and DCF model using data on the internet for many companies. Also, an additional learning outcome of this bootcamp is the Advanced Excel.
